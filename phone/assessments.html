PHQ-9 Depression Screening Required

Patient Health Questionnaire (PHQ-9) is a validated screening tool for depression symptoms.

  • 9 questions
  • Takes 2-3 minutes
  • Based on DSM-5 criteria
  • Measures mood and interest levels

GAD-7 Anxiety Screening Required

Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD-7) screens for anxiety symptoms and severity.

  • 7 questions
  • Takes 1-2 minutes
  • Measures worry and nervousness
  • Used in primary care settings
